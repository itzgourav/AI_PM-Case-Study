Verdict: AI failed Risk, Trust, and Economics tests. Proceeded with non-AI solution that preserves human agency.

Solution

Saved Text (Per-Chat)

Feature Design

  • Manual Selection: User-controlled saving of important messages
  • Zero AI Modification: No summarization, interpretation, or alteration
  • Native Integration: Accessible via chat profile view
  • Full Accountability: Users responsible for saved content
