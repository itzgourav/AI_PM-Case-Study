Trust Over Hype

Strategic AI Restraint in Messaging
AI Product Manager Case Study · WhatsApp
Executive Summary

Designing for Trust in High-Stakes Communication

WhatsApp users rely on messages as direct human intent. In this environment, AI errors don’t fail loudly — they fail silently.

This case study demonstrates a deliberate product decision: solving a real retrieval problem without introducing AI-driven risk.

Key Decision: Declined AI summarization. Shipped a non-AI “Saved Text” system that preserves user agency and trust.
Context

Why Messaging Is a High-Risk AI Surface

WhatsApp supports personal relationships, SMB coordination, and academic workflows. Errors are interpreted emotionally, not technically.

Problem

Retrieval, Not Creation

Users struggle to retrieve important messages from long, fast-moving chat streams. All messages are treated equally, regardless of importance.

Problem Type: Recall & coordination failure.
